Name: Bone block
Id: minecraft:bone_block
Hardness: 2
Source: Nether surface

Info:

The bone block is an generated block usually spawned in the nether as fossils, but can also be found in caves in the overworld. The bone block is and good source to bonemeal. Because you can craft 9 bonemeal per block.
