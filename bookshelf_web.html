Name: Bookshelf
Id: minecraft:bookshelf
Hardness: 1.5
Source: Crafted by books and wood

Info:

The bookshelf does only have 2 purposes, to upgrade your enchanting table and to decorate your base. The bookshelf block can be crafted with 6 wood planks and 3 books. Whats special about the block is that if you destroy it, it drops 3 books instead of the block it self.
