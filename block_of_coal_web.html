Name: Block of coal
Id: minecraft:block_of_coal
Hardness: 5
Source: Crafted by coal

Info:

The coal block is an block crafted by nine coal. It is often made to save space, but one block of coal can melt 80 items and 9 coal can only smelt 72. So coal blocks do actually last longer.
