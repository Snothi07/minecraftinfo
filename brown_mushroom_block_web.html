Name: Brown mushroom block
Id: minecraft:brown_mushroom_block
Hardness: 0.2
Source: Crafted by brown mushrooms

Info:

The brown mushroom block can be found in oak forests, dark oak forests and on mushroom islands. The block can also be crafted by 4 brown mushroms. The block is not very useful, but can be used in decoration.
