Name: Block of emerald
Id: minecraft:block_of_emerald
Hardness: 5
Source: Crafted by emeralds

Info:

The emerald block is a block crafted by 9 emeralds. This block is not very useful, but it is often made to save storage for other items. A lot of players like trading with villagers and get tons of emeralds, so being able to make them into blocks really helps.
